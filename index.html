LAWYER PRESENTATION

The Role of Lawyers in Society

INTRODUCTION

In every society, laws provide structure, fairness, and order. Lawyers ensure that these laws are applied correctly, protecting the rights of individuals and guiding organizations in legal matters.

Their role is vital for justice and stability.

WHO IS A LAWYER?

A lawyer is a trained professional licensed to practice law, advise clients, represent them in disputes, and draft legal documents.

Beyond courtroom advocacy, lawyers also prevent conflicts through negotiation and counseling.

ROLE IN JUSTICE SYSTEM

Lawyers act as crucial links between citizens and the legal system. They ensure fair trials, hold authorities accountable, and work to prevent misuse of power.

In doing so, they strengthen public trust in justice.

LAWYER’S RESPONSIBILITIES

A lawyer’s duty goes beyond winning cases. They must act with integrity, maintain confidentiality, and give honest advice.

Their ethical responsibility is to serve clients while respecting justice and truth.
